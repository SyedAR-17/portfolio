Mobility Hotspots in Helsinki
Visualizing the Most Visited Places Using Mobility Data
Project Details
This project leverages mobility data to identify and visualize the most frequently visited locations in Helsinki. By analyzing spatial patterns of human movement, the application helps uncover urban activity hotspots that are valuable for city planning, transportation analysis, and commercial site selection.
