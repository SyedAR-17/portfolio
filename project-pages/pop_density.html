Population Density of Pakistan
A 3D Geospatial Visualization of Demographic Concentration.
Project Details
In this project I worked to create a 3D visualization of Pakistan’s population density, highlighting spatial demographic patterns. Created using advanced cartographic design techniques, the map visualizes the population distribution across Pakistan. The national average population density stands at approximately 312 inhabitants per km². Population is heavily concentrated in the Indus River basin, stretching from Karachi through Multan, Lahore, and up to Islamabad. Urban centers like Karachi and Lahore exhibit the highest vertical peaks in the 3D density model.
