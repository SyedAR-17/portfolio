About Me
I am currently pursuing my Master's in Geoinformatics at Aalto University with a focus on remote sensing, Earth observation, and spatial data science. My expertise lies in working with satellite imagery and geospatial datasets to extract meaningful insights for environmental monitoring and sustainable development.
I have hands-on experience with tools such as Google Earth Engine, Python (GeoPandas, raster/ML libraries), ArcGIS Pro, and QGIS. I enjoy developing automated workflows for satellite image processing, applying machine learning to remote sensing data, and creating visualizations that make complex phenomena more understandable and impactful. I am currently open to opportunities in remote sensing, Earth observation applications, and geospatial analysis. If you’re working on innovative projects involving climate, disaster response, or sustainable land use, I’d be excited to connect and contribute.
