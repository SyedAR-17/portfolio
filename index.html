About Me
I am currently pursuing my Master's in Geoinformatics from Aalto University with a focus on GIS, remote sensing, and spatial data science. I have a strong foundation in tools like Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Python, and GeoPandas. I like turning spatial data into actionable insights and compelling visualizations. Whether it's creating automated scripts for satellite image processing or building interactive maps, I aim to make geospatial data accessible, impactful, and meaningful.
I am currently open to opportunities in GIS, remote sensing, or geospatial analysis. If you're working on innovative projects involving Earth observation, sustainability, or spatial modeling, I would love to connect and contribute.
